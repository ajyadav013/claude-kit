# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the claude-kit community
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, derogatory comments, or personal/political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers
via GitHub **Private vulnerability reporting** or a private issue on
<https://github.com/ajyadav013/claude-kit>. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
